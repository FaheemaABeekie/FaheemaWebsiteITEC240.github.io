About Us:

The purpose of this blog is to provide a platform dedicated to navigating life at the College of Science, Technology, and Applied Arts of Trinidad and Tobago (COSTAATT). Whether you're a new student applying to COSTAATT, registering for your first semester or a seasoned learner looking for guidance and assistance, this blog aims to do just that.

Some of the features this page will have are; a link to a webpage with contact information of multiple students in different fields who are willing to assist new students with their courses and assignments ie tutoring, step by step instructions on how to register for courses, the process of paying registration and what is needed, details on how to navigate the e-classroom, and of course my personal advice and experience at COSTAATT etc.

With this, our goals are to make college life, well life at COSTAATT much more understandable and easy to navigate because when we started, having a blog like this or someone to help us with these stuff would have been really helpful, but instead we had to struggle to figure them out. This website was created to create a welcoming community where students can find inspiration, motivation, and assistance in overcoming challenges they may encounter in school.
